Hvordan

The Infinity Jacket is using a set of solid-state battery pack with a new type of Lithium-ion rechargeable batteries, that gives 3x greater energy and has also removed the fire hazard associated with today’s batteries. Which makes the design flawless towards any hazards of having batteries in a jacket. This make sure that you could go longer without charging the jacket. It also enables the possibility to give the jacket a QI wireless charging device, which can charge any type of smartphone. You simply just put your phone in the front pocket of the jacket and you are good to go. If your phone does not support wireless charging, then we also have a usb input in the front pocket, so you could use your standard charger.

There will be two different ways to charge up your jacket. The main way will be to charge the jacket in any power socket by simply pulling out the charging cable from the side pocket of the jacket. The second way requires solar power, but this is a way to make the battery last even longer by using highly advanced technology to make flexible solar panels, that uses sun power to give additional battery time.

We have designed the jacket so the sunbeams go through the first layer of the jacket and getting absorbed into the flexible solar panels, but at the same time made sure that the jacket isolates the cold and keeping you warm. By having a three layered jacket we make sure that we keep the cool design by having the solar panels strategically placed around in the second layer of the jacket and the third layer is simply there to keep you warm and protect your upper body.

Vårt formål

We do not believe that this is just another gadget for the rich to embrace. Poor people in the western world will also need their phone to be charged. Infinity Wear wants to donate 10% of each sale to those in need living on the street in cold parts of the world. This money will be spent providing these people with their own infinity jacket. Keeping them warm and powered.

Even though not all the people living on the street owns a phone, we believe that technology is the future for every social layer of the society. In the western world it has almost become an necessity to have access to the internet. Not to check social media, but to use government institutions and banks. That is why our jacket will make the life easier. Not only for every day Joe, but also for the unlucky people of our society. With a bonus of keeping them warm.
